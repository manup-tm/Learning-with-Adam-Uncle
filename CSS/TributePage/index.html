Ada Lovelace

About Ada Lovelace

Ada Lovelace was an English mathematician and writer, chiefly known for her work on Charles Babbage's early mechanical general-purpose computer, the Analytical Engine. She is often regarded as the first computer programmer.

Key Contributions:

  • Developed the first algorithm intended to be processed by a machine.
  • Recognized the potential of computers beyond mere calculation.
  • Collaborated with Charles Babbage on the Analytical Engine.

For more information, visit her Wikipedia page.
